Projects

PlantScan – AI-Based Plant Species & Disease Detection System

PlantScan is an AI-powered web application designed to identify plant species and detect plant diseases from uploaded leaf images using advanced image analysis. The system provides accurate disease predictions along with treatment suggestions, preventive measures, and plant care recommendations to help users maintain healthy plants.

A unique feature of PlantScan is its location-based plant recommendation system. By entering a PIN code, users receive personalized suggestions for indoor and outdoor plants that are suitable for the climate and environmental conditions of their region. This helps users choose plants that are more likely to grow successfully in their local area.

The application also includes search history, an easy-to-use interface, and a responsive design, making it a practical solution for farmers, gardeners, plant enthusiasts, and beginners who want quick and reliable plant-related information.
